Master the Match-3 Mechanics and Become a Legend

  • Step 1: Swap and Match Jewels

    Match three or more identical jewels to clear them from the board.

    The more jewels you match, the bigger the rewards.

  • Step 2: Use Special Boosters

    Match four or more jewels to create explosive power-ups.

    Use bombs, lightning bolts, and cascading matches to clear challenging levels.

  • Step 3: Collect Stars & Earn Rewards

    Completing levels earns stars, which unlock special bonuses and prizes.

    Daily rewards and missions provide extra boosts for your adventure.

  • Step 4: Explore the Castle and Unlock New Challenges

    Progress through mystical castle levels, each introducing new obstacles and puzzles.

    Overcome barriers, unlock hidden artifacts, and uncover the castle's secrets.
